Frequently asked questions

Q: How do I find a lost item?
A: Go to the Search a Lost Item page, enter the description of the item in the search area, and browse the results to look for your item.

Q: How do I report a found item?
A: Go to the Report a Found Item page, fill the form with all the required details of the found item and submit it.

Q: What happens when I find my lost item?
A: Contact the person that uploaded the item you lost, verify your ownership, agree on a meeting place and time, and collect your item.

Q: Is there a reward for reporting items?
A: No. This platform is built on community support. Students report found items simply to help someone else on campus.

Q: Is there a fee to collect my items?
A: No. Claiming your lost item is completely free. Our goal is to make it easy for students to reunite with their belongings without any cost.

Q: How is the privacy of my data ensured?
A: Your data is securely stored and used only for communication related to lost and found items. The items uploaded are reviewed and may be removed if they breach our safety guidelines.
